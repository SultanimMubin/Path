### Notes

- ionic compound naming
    - cation first
    - anion
    - cation is named usually as the element
    - anion name
        - monotomic = -ide
        - two oxidation state
            - lower one = -ous
            - higher one = -ic
        - polyatomic
            - more oxygen atom = -ate
            - less oxygen atom = -ite
